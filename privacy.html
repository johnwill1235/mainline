Privacy Policy for Main Line App

Last updated: December 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Main Line app handles information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

Main Line uses your device's location services solely to determine your nearest SEPTA station and provide relevant train schedule information. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

How We Use Information

  • Location data is used only locally on your device to determine the nearest train station
  • No location data is sent to external servers or third parties
  • No personal information is collected or stored

Data Storage

  • All app preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No user data is transmitted to external servers
  • Train schedule data is retrieved directly from SEPTA's public API

Third-Party Services

This app uses SEPTA's public transit API to retrieve real-time schedule information. No personal data is shared with SEPTA or any other third parties.

Location Services

The Main Line app requests access to your device's location to:

  • Automatically determine your nearest SEPTA station
  • Provide relevant train schedules for your location
  • Enhance your user experience by showing the most relevant information

You can control location access through your iPhone's Settings app. The app will continue to function without location access, but you will need to manually select stations.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy. All app functionality happens locally on your device, ensuring your information stays private.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and within the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: support@mainlinetrain.xyz

Website: mainlinetrain.xyz

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns about location data, you can:

  • Disable location services for the Main Line app in your iPhone settings
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored preferences
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns
